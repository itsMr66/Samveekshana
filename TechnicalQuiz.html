Technical Quiz
Description of the Event:
Technical Quiz consists of 3 rounds:
1. Qualifying Round (in case more than 6 teams register)
   - Each team would be given a set of question paper.
   - Time Limit: 20 minutes.
   - Only 6 teams would be selected for stage rounds.
   - In case of a tie between two or more teams, further a set of questions would be asked for final selection.
2. The selected team shall have to appear for stage round.
   - Dumb Charades Round (technical words)  
 - Technical Round and General Round
- Buzzer Round

Rules for Quiz Competition:
- Registration fee for teams: ₹25 for S.G.B.I.T students, ₹50 for inter-college students.
- Only registered teams are eligible.
- A team should have only two team members.
- The participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or other electronic instruments.
- Audience shall not give any hints or clues to the participants.
- Replacement of any participant of a team is not allowed after registration.
- The decision of the judge will be the final and will not be subjected to any change.
