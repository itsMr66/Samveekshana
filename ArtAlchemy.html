Art Alchemy

Event Details:
- Team Formation: Each team consists of two artists, and each member is assigned an individual art sheet.
- Theme Announcement: Themes are announced at the beginning of the event, with each team member receiving a distinct theme.
- Painting Dynamics: The competition includes multiple 15-minute rounds. During each round, team members switch their art sheets and exchange themes for the next 15-minute interval. A total of 2 hours will be allocated to complete the artwork.
- Participants should carry their materials. Only art sheets will be provided.
- Any medium of paints is permissible, except pencil sketching.
- Plagiarism and the use of electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited.
