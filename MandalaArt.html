Mandala Art Competition

“Beauty lies in the eye of the beholder. Reflect it by the Mandalas we visualize”

Theme: Ocean
- No use of mobile phone, cheat sheet, pre-drawn, or printed designs allowed.
- Mandatory to incorporate the theme.
- Any color medium can be used.
- Time Limit: 1 hour 30 minutes.
- Participants must bring all the materials required for the Mandala competition such as regular pencils, colored pencils, graphite pencils, scale, compass, and eraser.
- Only an A4 sheet will be provided at the center.
- Participants must bring all the materials required for the Mandala competition from home.
- Incomplete works will not be accepted.

Judging Criteria:
1. Creativity
2. Originality of artwork
3. Adherence to theme
4. Complexity and neatness
5. Overall impact
