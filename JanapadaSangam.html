Janapada Sangam
[Folk Song]

- The duration of the performance will be a minimum of 3 and maximum of 5 minutes.
- The performance can be done solo or in a group.
- The team may present preferably those folk tunes which are recognized as folk tunes of different states.
- Songs performed must fall within the folk music genre. This includes traditional folk songs as well as contemporary folk music.
- Participants may accompany themselves with acoustic instruments commonly associated with folk music.

- Songs can be performed in any language.
- Judging criteria may include musicality, vocal/instrumental technique, stage presence, interpretation of the folk tradition, and overall performance quality.
- Participants must provide any necessary technical equipment or props required for their performance.

Event Organizers: 
- Will provide basic audio-visual equipment and stage setup, but participants should communicate any specific technical needs in advance.
- Only 20 teams limit in an event.
