Wave Hack

The "Wave Hack" is a unique competition that combines the thrill of a tech hunt with the precision of coding challenges and the high-stakes excitement of an ideation competition. Participants, including bright minds from the tech world, will navigate a series of mind-bending challenges, each designed to test their agility, adaptability, and technological prowess.

Team Specification:
- Participation in a team of 2 with a decent name is allowed.

Requirements:
- Each participant is requested to bring his/her laptop and can keep a paper and pen for rough work.

Total Duration:
- Round 1: 10 minutes for each team will be given to present their approach for solving the problem, and additional 10 minutes will be given to ideate about the problem given to us.
- Round 2: 1 hour (2 halves of 30 minutes each) will be given to each team, and an additional 15 minutes will be given for switching.
- Round 3: The time limit for the tech hunt is 2 hours.

Format:
- The competition will be conducted in 3 rounds:
  1. Maritime Mindstorm: Each team will be given a problem statement, and each team should present a valid approach to the solution. Each round will be an elimination round.
  2. Switch Coding: The round will be divided into two halves with 2 problem sets. In the first half, problem set 1 will be solved by member 1 and problem set 2 will be solved by member 2. In the second half, the problem set 1 will be solved by member 2 and the problem set 2 by member 1.
  3. Finding Memo: Tech-hunt. The rules for this round will be disclosed on the day of the event.
