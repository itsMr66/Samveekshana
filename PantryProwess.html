Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering 

Pantry Prowess
[Cook without Fire]

Team Size:
- Minimum: 1 participant
- Maximum: 2 participants

Ingredients & Equipment:
Ingredients: Participants must bring all the ingredients required for their dishes.
Restrictions:
- No pre-cooked, pre-cut, or processed ingredients are allowed.
- All ingredients must be brought raw and prepared on-site.
Equipment:
- Allowed: Knives, cutting boards, serving utensils, and non-electric food processors (e.g., mortars and pestles, graters).
- Not allowed: Any electrical appliances, heating devices, or open flames (e.g., blenders, microwaves, stoves).

Competition Duration:
- A total of 60 minutes will be given to prepare the dish(es).

Judging Criteria:
- Creativity and innovation in using ingredients without fire (50%)
- Taste and overall presentation of the dishes (50%)
- Judges' decision will be final.
