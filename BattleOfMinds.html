Battle Of Minds

Team Size: 2 participants in each team
Topics: Topics will be provided on the spot
Time Duration for Each Team: 15 minutes

General Instructions:
- No Shifting Viewpoints: The team supporting the motion must not shift its point of view.
- Evidence Required: All statements should be supported by evidence.
- Accuracy: Facts presented in a debate must be accurate.
- No New Points in Rebuttal: Focus on addressing opponent's arguments.



Debate Flow:
- Introduction (5 min/team): Briefly introduce yourselves, state your position, and highlight why your argument is important.
- Body (5 min/team):
  - Statement of Fact: Emphasize the central message of your argument.
  - Break Down the Thesis: Divide your argument into smaller, manageable points.
  - Support Each Point: Present factual statements backed by evidence (proof).
- Refutation (3 min/team): Address the opposing team's arguments.
- Conclusion (2 min/team): Summarize arguments and reiterate your statement.
