Competition Details:
- Team Composition: 2 members
- Teams per college: Unlimited
- Registration: Online

General Rules:
- Participants will be allotted a partner during the first round.
- Partners will be allotted on a first-come, first-serve basis.

Event Rules:
Round 1:
- Screen Test: Partners will be allotted and clues about their partner will be given.
- Participants must find their partner in a crowd.

Round 2:
- Details about this round will be revealed on the day of the competition itself.
- The top teams will proceed to the final round.
- Teams will not be changed in this round.

Final Round:
- Task Round: Teams will be put through a series of tasks testing their teamwork, creativity, and ability to think on their feet.
- Teams will be judged based on the speed/nature of completion of tasks.

Note:
- Rules may be altered at the discretion of the organizing team.
- All decisions of the organizing team and judges will be final and indisputable.
