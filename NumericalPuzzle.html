Department of Basic Science and Humanities

Numerical Puzzle

General Rules and Regulations:
- The event is going to be conducted in teams.
- Each team shall comprise of 3 members irrespective of semester and branch.
- Any misbehavior or indiscipline shall lead to strict disciplinary measures against the team.
- The event imposes a registration fee of Rs 200/- per team.
- Any use of electronic devices will lead to disqualification.
- Each team may be given a set of numerical puzzles to solve.
- Puzzles can vary in complexity and may include Sudoku, cross-sums, logic puzzles, and others.
- Participants must solve as many puzzles as possible within the allotted time.

Objective:
- The objective is to solve numerical puzzles within a specified time limit.

Rounds:
- Teams will be tasked with deciphering mysteries. 
- The mysteries shall lead to digital problems that need to be solved.
- The solutions of the digital problems will be in pen and paper style.
- This sequence iterates for up to 5 repetitions.
- The first team to decipher and solve all that is given will be crowned.

Scoring:
- Points are typically awarded for each correctly solved puzzle.
- In some cases, bonus points may be awarded for completing puzzles within a certain time frame or for solving particularly challenging puzzles.

Judging:
- Judges or event organizers verify the solutions provided by participants.
- In case of discrepancies or disputes, the decision of the judges is usually final.

Prizes:
- Prizes may be awarded to the top-performing individuals or teams, based on their scores.
