COD [CALL OF DUTY]

Team Composition:
- Each team must consist of 5 members.
- Players cannot be part of more than one team in the tournament.

Eligibility:
- Participants must have a valid Call of Duty Mobile account.
- All team members must be registered and approved before the tournament starts.
- Players must adhere to the game's terms of service and community guidelines.

Game Settings:
- Game Mode: Arena.
- Matches will be played using official Call of Duty Mobile multiplayer maps.
- Game mode rotation will include Search and Destroy, Hardpoint, and Team Deathmatch.
- The tournament organizer will announce specific maps and game modes for each round or match.

Equipment and Loadouts:
- Players are allowed to use any equipment, weapons, and loadouts available in the game.
- Use of cheats, hacks, or exploits is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disqualification.

Match Procedures:
- Matches will be scheduled according to the tournament's official schedule.
- Teams must be ready to start matches on time and follow the match-ups provided by the tournament organizer.
- In the case of connection issues or technical difficulties, teams should contact the organizer immediately for resolution.

Code of Conduct:
- All participants must adhere to the tournament's code of conduct, which includes respecting opponents, avoiding toxic behavior, and promoting a positive gaming environment.

Note: Organizers can alter the rules as per the situation. Teams must be ready in any situation. Good luck!
