Team Size:
- Minimum: 1 participant
- Maximum: 2 participants

Presentation Guidelines:
Presentation Duration:
- Each team will be allotted 15 minutes for their presentation, followed by a 5-minute question and answer session with the judges.

Content:
- Presentations should effectively cover:
  - Introduction: Briefly introduce yourselves, your team, and the topic of your research.
  - Problem Statement: Clearly define the research/problem statement.
  - Methodology: Explain the methods used to conduct the project/research (data collection, analysis techniques, etc.).
  - Results & Discussion: Present findings, analyze the data, and discuss the significance of research.
  - Conclusion: Summarize key findings and potential applications of research.

Judging Criteria:
- Clarity and Content (40%): Logical flow of information, effective communication of research objectives, findings, and conclusions.
- Technical Accuracy (30%): Sound research methodology, proper data analysis, and accurate presentation of results.
- Presentation Skills (20%): Delivery, clarity, audience engagement, and effective use of visual aids.
- Question & Answer Session (10%): Ability to answer questions confidently, in-depth knowledge of the topic, and address queries from the judges effectively.

Awards:
- Winners and runners-up will be recognized and awarded prizes.
