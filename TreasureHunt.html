Competition Details:
- A game where teams search for hidden treasure using clues.
- Each team must have at least 2 members and no more than 4 members.
- Rounds consist of specific sets of clues or challenges.
- Teams must collaborate and work together to solve clues and challenges.
- Each team member must take turns leading the team in a round.
- Time limits may be set for each round.
- Points awarded based on team performance in each round determine the overall winner.
- Teams must follow instructions and additional rules set by the organizer.
- Fair play and sportsmanship are essential.
- Cheating or unsportsmanlike behavior will result in disqualification.
- Team members should support and encourage each other.
- Decisions by the judges are final.
